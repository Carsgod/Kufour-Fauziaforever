Our Story

How We Met

The beautiful love story we celebrate today began in 2024, during our postgraduate studies in International Relations at the University of Ghana, LECIAD. It was a rainy afternoon, and Fauzia had stepped out to collect her lunch from a dispatch rider, just as Kufuor was hurrying back from his car to escape the downpour. We quite literally bumped into each other at the faculty entrance, a simple moment made unforgettable by the rain's embrace.

A Friendship to Remember

On countless occasions, we showed deep commitment to nurturing our friendship from supporting each other with academic assignments, preparing reading summaries, and sharing presentation notes, to studying side by side for exams. We exchanged beautiful thoughts, agreed and disagreed on issues, and most importantly, always cheered each other on. These cherished moments gave birth to our special nicknames: Marco (Kufuor) and Polo (Fauzia).

Celebrating Our Moments

We always made it a point to celebrate even the smallest wins. From the excitement of completing weekly lectures and engaging in extracurricular activities, to marking special occasions like birthdays, promotions, family milestones, and anniversaries — we embraced them all with love and joy. Whether at the beach, the cinema, visiting family, or dining at our favorite restaurant, Ocean Basket, where we created many of our fondest memories, every moment was special because we shared it together.

The Proposal

Days of friendship grew into weeks of unforgettable memories, and months of two imperfect souls writing their own forever story. One evening, after a beautiful night out, just before we drove off, Marco gently reached for my left hand. Looking straight into my eyes, he spoke softly: "This is a priceless gift. My grandmother gave it to me before she passed on, telling me to present it to the woman I would take as my wife. Today, I give it to you. Will you be my partner, my wife, the mother of my children, and everything my heart desires?" With all my heart, I said "Yes!" — emphatically and forever.

Journey Forever

A journey that began as friendship—study partners, best friends, and each other's greatest supporters—now blossoms into a beautiful new beginning as husband and wife. With the love, guidance, and blessings of God and our parents, we step into this next chapter. So, help us God.
